Frequently Asked Questions

Is Vaad free to use?
Yes. Basic search and dashboard functions are available for free. For heavy usage, bulk CNR refreshing, and daily cause lists, we offer affordable Pro and Supreme tiers starting at just ₹99/month.
Is my financial data safe?
Absolutely. We use Google Firebase with robust security rules. Your ledger is strictly private, synced only to your account, and hosted securely on servers located in Mumbai, India.
Can I access my dashboard on my laptop?
Yes! Because Vaad is cloud-synced, you can log in to the Web App on your laptop in the chamber, and instantly see the exact same dashboard you have on your phone in the courtroom.
How do I install the Direct APK?
Download the APK file using the button above. Open the file on your Android device. Your phone may ask you to allow "Install from Unknown Sources" in your settings. Once allowed, the installation will complete in seconds.
