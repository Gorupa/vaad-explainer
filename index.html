World's First

Secured by zkTLS Cryptography

ecourt.js is the first Indian legal data library to implement Zero-Knowledge Transport Layer Security (zkTLS).

What does that mean for you? It acts like an unbreakable digital notary. When Vaad fetches your case, zkTLS creates a mathematical receipt proving the data came directly from the government server and was never altered by us or any middleman. 100% verifiable truth.
