Frequently Asked Questions

Is my financial data safe?
Absolutely. We use Google Firebase with robust security rules. Your ledger is strictly private, synced only to your account, and hosted securely on cloud servers.
What languages does Vaad AI support?
Vaad AI understands and replies in the language you use. You can ask questions in English, pure Hindi, or Hinglish (Hindi written in English letters). It will adapt to you perfectly.
Can I access my dashboard on my laptop?
Yes! Because Vaad is cloud-synced, you can log in to the Web App on your laptop in the chamber, and instantly see the exact same dashboard you have on your phone in the courtroom.
